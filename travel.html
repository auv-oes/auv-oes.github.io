Travel Info

Official Letters of invitation for visa purpose

Official letters of invitation for visa applications are available upon request. These letters do not and cannot commit the Organizing Committee to any financial obligation.

COVID-19 & Safe Management

AUV will adhere to prevailing safe-management measures and regulations in place in Singapore at the time of the event. Currently, Singapore has relaxed its COVID-19 regulations to DORSCON Yellow status. Refer to the Singapore Safe Travel for details on current regulations.

Visa regulations

Many nationalities can enter Singapore without a visa. Entry permit duration depends on nationality and entry point. Refer to the Immigration and Checkpoints Authority for current guidelines.

Travelling to Singapore

Singapore is one of Southeast Asia's largest aviation hubs, so for global travellers the easiest way to enter Singapore is by air. In addition to Singapore Airlines and SilkAir, Singapore is also home to low-cost carriers, Scoot and Jetstar Asia.

Travelling within Singapore

Getting around Singapore is easy. The public transportation system is extremely easy to use and taxis are reasonably priced. All public transport systems in Singapore use the EZ-link contactless card or a Nets Flash Pay card. You can store value on it and use it on trains (MRT) and city buses. The card can be bought at MRT stations can be "topped up" in increments at the farecard vending machines or 7-Eleven stores. Taxis use meters and are reasonably priced. Mobile apps like Ryde, TADA, Gojek, and Grab are handy in getting Taxis. Find more information on the Wikitravel SG website
